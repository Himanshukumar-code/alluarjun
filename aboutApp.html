ClickReal!

ClickReal is a photo-sharing application that allows users to post one photo per day to show their followers what they are doing in real-time.The ClickReal app focuses on authenticity and asks users to be real. Because the app allows everyone to post only once a day, users can avoid being on the application for multiple hours throughout the day, making the app stand out from social media platforms currently dominant in the space.

What is ClickReal?

The social media application available for download on Android and available on Google PlayStore is developed durig college project by Four students Himanshu Kumar, Karina Das,Rahul Chatterjee and Shreya Sinha The app encourages users to share who they are in the moment.
